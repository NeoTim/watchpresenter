Control with your phone

That's it! Now you can control the presentation with your phone

Simply open the WatchPresenter app on your phone and use the volume buttons to control it.

Just press the volume up button for next slide and volume down for the previous one.

Using the volume buttons while using WatchPresenter will not modify the riger volume, so you don't have to worry about that!

You can even switch the screen off if you want to save battery and avoid distractions.

Once you are done, just dismiss the notification that appears on the phone to switch the app off
